Executive Summary

HCP Pros spend hundreds of dollars a month on shared data leads that don't convert ‚Äî they're in the field when homeowners call, competitors answer first, and Pros pay for leads they never had a real shot at winning. We propose enabling Pros to purchase exclusive inbound phone calls directly inside HCP, fulfilled automatically by CSR AI for guaranteed 24/7 answer and booking ‚Äî turning call buying from an external workaround into a native, high-ROI acquisition channel. Revenue Model

The "Marketing Employee" Framing

At $49‚Äì59/month, this is not a software line item ‚Äî it's the cost of adding a dedicated marketing employee to the Pro's team. A single incremental booked job at ~$200‚Äì300 average revenue pays for 4‚Äì6 months of the product. The ROI conversation is not "does the software cost too much?" ‚Äî it's "did you get at least one more job this month?" For an owner-operator in the field, that answer is almost always yes.

Warm Cohort ‚Äî The Primary Audience

~7,000 Pros received at least one lead via Job Inbox in January 2026. They're already lead buyers inside HCP ‚Äî this is an upgrade motion from shared data leads to exclusive inbound calls, not cold acquisition of the 55K Pro base. Additionally, ~2,000 Pros are currently on CSR AI (3.6% baseline attach rate), giving us a clean starting point for the forced-attach kicker: ~96% of call buyers are net-new CSR AI attaches with no double-counting.
