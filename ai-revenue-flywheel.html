The AI Revenue Flywheel

Six PM bets, one customer journey. How AI becomes the connective tissue across every stage from discovery to re-engagement ‚Äî and the case for prioritizing hard outcomes over feature count.

Author: Chris Kosednar Date: Feb 23, 2026 Status: Working draft Team: Andre ¬∑ Sani ¬∑ AJ ¬∑ Kasia ¬∑ Anyssa ¬∑ Natalie

The Thesis

HCP's six PM bets aren't independent features ‚Äî they're sequential stages of a single homeowner journey. A Pro who gets discovered through a strong GBP listing, captures the inquiry through OLB or inbound call, converts it with a sharp AI-drafted estimate, and follows up automatically is running a fundamentally different business than one who doesn't. The individual features matter less than closing the loop between them.

The constraint isn't capability ‚Äî it's conversion. At every handoff (discovery ‚Üí inquiry, inquiry ‚Üí estimate, estimate ‚Üí booking, job ‚Üí review), there's revenue falling out of the funnel. Each PM bet we're running maps to a specific leak. CSR AI is the connective tissue ‚Äî the AI layer that should eventually handle every real-time communication touchpoint so the Pro never has to be present for the conversation to happen.

Two specific conversion problems are not yet clearly owned and deserve focused attention: (1) AI-powered estimate improvement ‚Äî using job history, competitive context, and sales psychology to send better estimates ‚Äî and (2) automated estimate follow-up ‚Äî a multi-touch AI sequence to get unaccepted estimates across the line. Both have well-understood economics and can move revenue meaningfully without requiring new product surface.

Unlocked White Space

High-value opportunities not yet owned ‚Äî need a decision
Highest Confidence
Estimate Follow-Up Automation
Likely owner: Kasia + Andre ¬∑ Stage 04
A Pro sends an estimate. The homeowner doesn't respond. Currently: nothing happens unless the Pro manually follows up. 40-60% of unaccepted estimates go cold without any contact. AI knows the estimate exists, the amount, the service type, and how long it's been sitting. This is a solved problem with automation sequences + AI-drafted messages.
Business case: If 20% of cold estimates are recovered by automated follow-up at an avg ticket of $400, a cohort of 10K pros sending 2 estimates/month = ~$16M/year in recovered Pro revenue. HCP's take rate on recovered jobs (through bookings) has a clear multiplier.
Strong Signal
AI-Powered Estimate Quality
Likely owner: Kasia or new bet ¬∑ Stage 03
Estimates are the most important document a Pro sends ‚Äî but they're built manually, inconsistently, and without any sales strategy behind them. AI has the job transcript, Pro's past winning estimates, competitive price benchmarks, and social proof data. It can draft better estimates than most Pros write themselves ‚Äî and apply proven sales tactics (scarcity, urgency, anchoring, social proof) to improve acceptance rate.
Business case: If estimate acceptance rate improves from 30% to 40% (a 33% lift), and HCP has 50K active Pros each sending 10 estimates/month, the revenue impact is significant. Harder to measure directly but clear in LTV and booking volume.
Accelerator Bet
Inbound Call Marketplace
Sponsor: Chris K ¬∑ Stage 02
Pros buy exclusive inbound phone calls from a provider (eLocal), routed to CSR AI for 100% answer rate and booking. Solves the shared-lead failure loop entirely: exclusive, live, AI-answered. The 7K warm cohort (CSR AI + active lead spend) is the addressable market for Phase 1.
Business case: $2.8M ARR base case at 25% cohort attach. $5.1M at 35%. Requires CSR AI spam billing fix as prerequisite. Three implementation options scoped. ‚Üí Full brief
The Connective Tissue

CSR AI is the infrastructure, not just a feature

Every communication touchpoint in the customer journey ‚Äî answering a call, replying to an inbox message, following up on an estimate, closing a campaign response, recovering a dropped booking, requesting a review ‚Äî is a place where the Pro needs to be present but can't be. CSR AI's job is to be present for all of them.

Andre's "AI Manager v3" direction (making CSR AI an agent) is the right architectural bet: an AI that doesn't just answer calls but manages the entire communication relationship on the Pro's behalf. The value of each other PM's work compounds when CSR AI is the handoff layer ‚Äî OLB captures the lead and hands it to CSR AI, campaigns send the message and CSR AI handles the reply, the estimate is sent and CSR AI follows up.

The Measurement Priority

Before making the business case for any of these bets to leadership, the highest-value diagnostic is: what is our current estimate acceptance rate, and what is our CSR AI booking rate? Those are the two numbers that anchor the revenue model for everything in stages 03 and 02 respectively. If we don't know them, we're arguing with intuition. If we do know them, we can model the value of a 10-point improvement in each with confidence.
