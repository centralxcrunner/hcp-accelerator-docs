The AI Revenue Flywheel

Six PM bets, one customer journey. How AI becomes the connective tissue across every stage from discovery to re-engagement — and the case for prioritizing hard outcomes over feature count.

Author: Chris Kosednar Date: Feb 23, 2026 Status: Working draft Team: Andre · Sani · AJ · Kasia · Anyssa · Natalie

The Thesis

HCP's six PM bets aren't independent features — they're sequential stages of a single homeowner journey. A Pro who gets discovered through a strong GBP listing, captures the inbound call through CSR AI, books an estimate appointment, shows up and sends a sharp estimate, follows up until it's signed, completes the actual work, collects a review, and pushes it back to GBP is running a fundamentally different business than one who doesn't. The individual features matter less than closing the loop between them.

The constraint isn't capability — it's conversion. At every handoff (discovery → inbound → CSR AI books appt → pre-appt comms → appt + estimate → estimate signed → work complete → review → GBP updated), there's revenue falling out of the funnel. Each PM bet we're running maps to a specific leak. CSR AI is the connective tissue — the AI layer that should eventually handle every real-time communication touchpoint so the Pro never has to be present for the conversation to happen.

Two specific conversion problems are not yet clearly owned and deserve focused attention: (1) AI-powered estimate improvement — using job history, competitive context, and sales psychology to send better estimates — and (2) automated estimate follow-up — a multi-touch AI sequence to get unaccepted estimates across the line. Both have well-understood economics and can move revenue meaningfully without requiring new product surface.

Go Do This First

One priority per PM — and where it might be worth starting instead
Andre Ottoni
HCPA / CSR AI
Go Do This First
Improve CSR AI booking rate — call quality, trust, and prompt improvements to convert more intent-to-book calls into appointments.
60% of ITB calls (~3,375/day) walk away without booking. Every percentage point gained = ~56 more appointments per day.
Consider starting here instead
Before changing any prompts, do a 2-week transcript analysis to categorize WHY ITB calls don't book — scheduling? price? trust? The AB framework only works once you have a specific hypothesis. Build the experiment around the most common failure mode, not a guess.
Sani Djaya
Campaigns / Marketing Hub → GBP
Go Do This First
When a job is marked complete with photos, automatically push those photos to the Pro's GBP listing as a post.
Real job photos are GBP's highest local ranking signal. Pros almost never update GBP manually. Automates the highest-impact SEO action with zero Pro effort. 25% of pros (~X) already have GBP connected.
Also do this now
With only 25% GBP connection rate today, launch a "connect your GBP" prompt inside HCP before the auto-push feature ships. Double the addressable market first — the feature is only as valuable as the connection rate.
Kasia Szenajch
OLB + Pipeline
Go Do This First
Improve OLB conversion through obvious tweaks — reduce form fields, simplify the flow, lower friction at every step.
Every lead from website, GBP, and campaigns passes through OLB. A 10pp conversion lift = hundreds of additional bookings per month across the Pro base.
Consider starting here instead
Pull OLB funnel drop-off data before picking the tweak. "Obvious" without data often misses the actual problem. Identify the single field with the highest abandon rate, remove it, ship as an A/B test. Don't redesign the whole form — change one thing and measure.
Anyssa Barbour
Websites + Reviews
Go Do This First
Get AI sending personalized SMS review requests automatically — customized by job type, triggered immediately at job completion.
Review count and recency are the top GBP ranking signals. Automated personalized requests require zero Pro action and turn every completed job into compounding reputation capital.
Consider starting here instead
Timing beats copy. When does the review request go out today? Move the trigger to immediately at job completion first — before adding AI personalization. A generic SMS at the right moment outperforms perfect copy sent 24 hours late.
AJ McCraw
Job Inbox
Go Do This First
Connect Job Inbox to CSR AI — every new inbound lead gets an immediate outbound SMS within 30 seconds, no manual action required from the Pro.
Sub-1-min response = +391% conversion. Every lead sitting unresponded for 10+ minutes loses 400% conversion potential. The inbox needs to become an active response machine, not a passive bucket.
Align with Andre first
CSR AI text reply to leads from lead forms is already in design (Jan 29 — Andre's work). These are the same surface. Agree on ownership before building: AJ owns inbox routing trigger, Andre's team owns CSR AI conversation logic. Don't ship two separate SMS systems.
Natalie
Campaigns
Go Do This First
Set up an agentic campaign approach — AI identifies the right cohort, sends the right offer, and CSR AI responds to all replies to close appointments.
Campaigns with AI cohort targeting + CSR AI close turn a static broadcast into a two-way revenue conversation. Every campaign reply is a potential appointment.
Consider starting here instead
Run one well-segmented manual campaign first. Build the attribution model (campaign → reply → appt → job) before making it agentic. Also: the CSR AI reply dependency (Andre) needs to be live before sending campaigns — otherwise you're generating leads with no close mechanism.

The Connective Tissue

CSR AI is the infrastructure, not just a feature

Every communication touchpoint in the customer journey — answering a call, replying to an inbox message, following up on an estimate, closing a campaign response, recovering a dropped booking, requesting a review — is a place where the Pro needs to be present but can't be. CSR AI's job is to be present for all of them.

Andre's "AI Manager v3" direction (making CSR AI an agent) is the right architectural bet: an AI that doesn't just answer calls but manages the entire communication relationship on the Pro's behalf. The value of each other PM's work compounds when CSR AI is the handoff layer — OLB captures the lead and hands it to CSR AI, campaigns send the message and CSR AI handles the reply, the estimate is sent and CSR AI follows up.
